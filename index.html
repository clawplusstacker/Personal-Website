About Me


My name is Colby Beach and I am 18 years old from Albany, NY. I have been interested in creating different things for as long as I can remember. I have been programming and making YouTube videos since 2014, and writing music with my brother in our band "IGNIS" since 2017. With YouTube, I have gained over 1600 subscribers, and with IGNIS have released two albums, three singles, two EP's, and have accumulated over 20,000 streams on Apple Music alone. I hope to continue these interests as hobbies while in college studying computer science with an aspiration to be a software developer in the future. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, Java, Python and Swift/SwiftUI, Realm, Firebase and Firestore. This site is a hub for all of my interests as well as updates with my progress in all three fields. If you need to get a hold of me be sure to click on the "Contact" page above and fill out the form!
